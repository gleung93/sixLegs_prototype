Q

Are they safe?

Yes! Insects are as safe if not safer than other animal foods.
Because insect biology is so far from humans they are actually less likely to transmit diseases to us. As with any raw food, precautions are taken to make sure that the food is properly stored, transported and cooked.

What are the health benefits?

Health benefits are very specific to the insect species
Generally they are high in protein and rich in vitamins and minerals. Since we usually consume insects whole we get a lot more nutrients than traditional meats where we only eat the muscle tissue.

Q

Where do your insects come from?

We only source the best
At Six Legs we work with insect farmers around the country to make sure you always get the highest quality food possible. While we'd love to get everthing locally this isn't yet possible because the industry is still in it's infancy.

When looking for a source we make sure that the bugs are raised specifically for human consumption, housed in an ethical environment and free of any chemical cleaners or preservatives.
