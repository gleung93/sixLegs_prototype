Q

Why eat insects?

1) They are delicious. We consult professional chefs to make sure that our dishes are as tasty as possible. And just like with any food some people have different prefences - that's why we always have a diverse range of menu itmes to choose from.
Explore our menu

2) They are good for the environment Utlizing insects would reduce our usage of water, farm land and even cut down co2 emissions.
Learn more

3) They are good for your health Insects are high in protein and packed with nutrients like iron, calcium, vitamin b12, and omega 3 fatty acids.
Learn more

4) Most people eat them 80% of the world's nations eat them and are even considered a delicacy among many cultures.
Learn more

5) Help create an affordable source of protein Increasing demand for edible insects would create a market that could help feed a growing world population
